Our Story

Belle's Bookstore and Coffee is a longtime dream come to life based loosely on the library, "Nevermore", from the Disney Classic, Beauty and the Beast. Our quaint location was chosen to fulfill a life long dream of a bookstore that would house old and new books and double as coffee shop to be a safe place, serving guests from around the world. This concept was dreamed up and brought to you by book-lover, Disney enthusiast and coffee connoisseur, Isabella Walker.

We proudly serve those with their 'nose stuck in a book'!
